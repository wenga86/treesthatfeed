Trees That Feed Foundation
Feeding People and Benefiting the Environment
 

Please donate to Trees That Feed Foundation

We want to feed hungry people in tropical countries and benefit our global environment. We hope to plant millions of food-bearing trees.

US $15.00 funds a robust breadfruit tree planted and growing to feed people for 50 to 100 years! $250.00 provides valuable equipment! $1,500 provides trees, equipment, and training to a community.

Help us with your contribution, large or small!

Our mission will be accomplished by planting trees that require low inputs and can be grown in small farms, field margins and urban backyards. The goals are to replace at least one meal per week and to reduce per-calorie meal cost by 20% compared to imported grain.
