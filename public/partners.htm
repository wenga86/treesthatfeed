Organizations Working With Us

Three Angels Childrens' Relief

President Eric Helgemo has built a nursery in Port au Prince, Haiti, to grow out breadfruit and other fruit trees, for distribution or sale locally. Three Angels also does other excellent work caring for and teaching children without a family.

National Tropical Botanical Garden, Hawaii

Breadfruit Institute Director, Dr. Diane Ragone, has identified cultivars with a range of characteristics including lengthy growing seasons.

Jamaica Ministry of Agriculture, Kingston, Jamaica

The Ministry has agreed to distribute and supervise planting and care at local farms. Paul Virtue, TTFF Executive Director, is in contact.

Rural Agricultural Development Agency Jamaica

Distributor of TTFF trees to farmers.

Jeffrey Town Farmers Association

This is a strong vibrant community that has developed a community center, an agro processing unit and a community radio station.They are planting as many breadfruit trees as they are able. They work towards making farming an honorable and profitable occupation so creating a secure community.

It's a holistic approach to bringing our community forward," Ivy Gordon Secretary of Jeffrey Town Farmers Association told the Observer.

Ivy and Joe Gordon of the Association came to Chicago to help TTFF with the Chicago Flower and Garden show in March 2011. They worked very long hours each day educating Chicagoans about the benefits of the breadfruit and the Agro Forest system that their community works on tirelessly.

They are the winners of the 2006 Michael Manley Foundation Award for Community Self-Reliance. Click here to more read more from the Jamaica Gleaner.

Click here to listen to their radio station Jet 88.7 FM.
